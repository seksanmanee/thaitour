How does location sharing work?

Share your location with all your friends. If you and a friend both added each other on IonicBucket, you can choose to share your locations with each other.

Your location on the map only updates when you have open IonicBucket. So you don't have to worry about your location being updated in the background or anything.

And also don't worry you can change this location option from Settings.
